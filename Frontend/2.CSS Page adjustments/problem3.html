1. Artificial Intelligence (AI): Artificial Intelligence is a branch of computer science that focuses on creating machines and systems that can think, learn, and make decisions like humans. It is used in many areas such as virtual assistants, self-driving cars, healthcare, and online recommendations. AI helps make tasks faster and more accurate, improving efficiency in daily life.
2. Climate Change: Climate change refers to long-term changes in temperature and weather patterns on Earth, mainly caused by human activities like burning fossil fuels and deforestation. It leads to problems such as global warming, melting ice caps, rising sea levels, and extreme weather events. Reducing pollution and using clean energy can help control climate change.
3. Renewable Energy: Renewable energy comes from natural sources like sunlight, wind, water, and heat from the Earth, which are constantly replenished. Unlike fossil fuels, renewable energy is environmentally friendly and reduces pollution. It is becoming more important as the world looks for sustainable ways to meet energy needs and protect the environment.
