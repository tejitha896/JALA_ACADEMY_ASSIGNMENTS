All About Cookies 🍪

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small, sweet baked treats made with ingredients like flour, sugar, butter, and eggs. They come in many flavors such as chocolate chip, oatmeal, and peanut butter.

Popular Types of Cookies

  • Chocolate Chip Cookies
  • Oatmeal Cookies
  • Butter Cookies
  • Shortbread Cookies
  • Peanut Butter Cookies

Ingredients Used

Common ingredients include flour, sugar, butter, eggs, baking soda, and flavorings like vanilla or chocolate.
