Artificial Intelligence (AI)
  • Machines can learn and think like humans.
  • Used in apps like chatbots and voice assistants.
  • Helps in healthcare, finance, and education.
  • Can automate repetitive tasks.
  • Improves over time with more data.
Internet of Things (IoT)
  • Connects devices to the internet.
  • Devices can communicate with each other.
  • Used in smart homes and smart cities.
  • Helps monitor and control devices remotely.
  • Improves convenience and efficiency.
Blockchain
  • Stores data in secure digital blocks.
  • Information cannot be easily changed.
  • Works without a central authority.
  • Used in cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin.
  • Provides transparency and security.
